Bootcamp

A 3-days event where experts in Design Thinking/ UX Design, Software Development and local mentors lead teams of talents to solve problems and evolve innovative solutions.

Talents will use practical skills learned during the training sessions on ideation, UX, problem solving, soft skills, and hard skills (in web, mobile, software development, data analytics and digital marketing) to create functional minimum viable products on DEMO Day.
